Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 9, 2026

Thank you for choosing to be part of our community by using Mimic - Intelligent Mock Engine ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

1. Core Privacy Philosophy

Mimic is built with an offline-first, privacy-by-design approach. We do not harvest, collect, or upload your biometric data, facial geometry, or camera feeds to any external servers. All motion tracking and processing happens entirely on-device.

2. What Data We Process

  • Camera Data: The App requires access to your device's camera to function. The camera feed is analyzed in real-time using local machine learning models (such as Google ML Kit and MediaPipe) to extract motion data, facial expressions, and body poses.
  • Biometric Tracking: The algorithms calculate a mathematical representation of your movement. This data is never used to identify you personally.

3. How Your Data is Used and Stored

  • On-Device Processing: The mathematical tracking data exists temporarily in your device's RAM to drive the motion capture system.
  • Local File Storage: If you choose to record a session, the motion data (e.g., BVH or JSON files) is saved strictly to your device's local storage. We do not sync these files to the cloud.

4. Advertising and Monetization

To support the development of Mimic, we display advertisements within the App. We use third-party advertising networks (such as Google AdMob) to serve these ads. These networks may collect and use information about your device, such as your Advertising ID, IP address, and general location data, to provide personalized ads and analyze ad performance.

Please note that the biometric tracking data and camera feeds are never shared with advertisers.

5. App Permissions

Mimic requires the following permissions to operate properly:

  • Camera: Strictly for optical motion and facial capture.
  • Microphone / Record Audio: Used exclusively for local lip-sync viseme extraction. The audio feed is analyzed mathematically on-device to create mouth shapes and is never recorded or transmitted.
  • Storage: To save your recorded animation files (BVH/JSON).
  • Network / Internet: Used for local-network UDP transmission (VMC OSC broadcast to your desktop), to load advertisements, and to verify ad network connectivity. No tracking or payload data leaves your private LAN via our engine.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses local SDKs (such as Google ML Kit) to process data on-device. We also integrate third-party advertising SDKs (e.g., Google AdMob) which may collect data as outlined in their respective privacy policies.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, or if you need to request support regarding your data, please contact the developer through our designated app store support channels.
